R A M A N A T H A P U R A M

Ramanathapuram is a district located in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. The district is known for its historical and cultural significance, and it is home to several famous temples that attract devotees and tourists. Explore the divine ambiance and architectural marvels of these renowned temples in Ramanathapuram and Rameshwaram.

RAMANATHASWAMY TEMPLE, RAMESHWARAM

Ramanathaswamy Temple is one of the most famous temples in Rameshwaram. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is believed to be one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples in India. The temple has a beautiful architecture with intricately carved pillars and corridors. It is also known for its large and sacred water tank called "Agni Theertham," where devotees take a holy dip before entering the temple.

KOTHANDARAMASWAMY TEMPLE, RAMESHWARAM

Kothandaramaswamy Temple is an ancient temple located in Rameshwaram. It is dedicated to Lord Rama and is believed to be the place where Lord Rama performed the Pattabhishekam ceremony for Vibhishana, the brother of Ravana. The temple is known for its stunning carvings depicting scenes from the epic Ramayana.

GANDHAMADHANA PARVATHAM, RAMESHWARAM

Gandhamadhana Parvatham is a hillock located in Rameshwaram. It is believed to be the place where Lord Hanuman took a leap to Lanka to search for Goddess Sita. The hillock offers a panoramic view of the island and has a temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.

PANCHAMUKHA ANJANEYAR, RAMESHWARAM

The Five-faced Hanuman Temple is located in Rameshwaram and is dedicated to Lord Hanuman. The idol of Lord Hanuman in this temple is believed to have been installed by Lord Rama himself. The temple is known for its unique idol with five faces of Lord Hanuman.

ADI JAGANATHA PERUMAL TEMPLE, SETHU KARAI

Adi Jagannatha Perumal Temple is located in Sethu Karai, near Rameshwaram. It is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu. The temple has beautiful architecture and is an important pilgrimage site for Vaishnavites.

UTHIRAKOSAMANGAI TEMPLE, RAMANATHAPURAM

Uthirakosamangai Temple is an ancient temple located in Ramanathapuram. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its architectural beauty and historical significance. The temple has a sacred tank called "Teppakulam," where festivals are celebrated.

NAVABHASANA VINAYAGAR TEMPLE, RAMANATHAPURAM

Navabhashana Vinayagar Temple is located in Ramanathapuram. It is dedicated to Lord Vinayagar (Ganesha) and is known for its unique idol made of nine types of medicinal herbs. The temple has a peaceful ambiance and is visited by devotees seeking blessings.

THIRUPULLANI TEMPLE, RAMESHWARAM

Thirupullani Temple is an ancient temple located in Ramanathapuram. It is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is believed to be the place where Lord Rama performed the last rites for Jatayu, the divine bird who sacrificed his life while trying to save Goddess Sita from Ravana.
