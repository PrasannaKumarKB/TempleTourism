HISTORY

Punnainallur Mariamman temple is located near Thanjavur. This temple is most powerful and has many incredible secrets. This temple was built in the year 1680 by a Maratha ruler. This temple is an existing example of Dravidian and Maratha architecture. People from all over the world visit is temple for good health and prosperous life.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Thanjavur is Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ), located approximately 58 kilometers away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Thanjavur. Once in Thanjavur, you can further hire a taxi or take a local bus to reach the Punnai Nallur Mariamman Temple.

By Train: Thanjavur has its own railway station called Thanjavur Junction, which is well-connected to major cities in India. From the railway station, you can hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or take a local bus to reach the Punnai Nallur Mariamman Temple. The temple is located about 8 kilometers from the railway station.

By Road: Thanjavur is well-connected by road and has good bus connectivity. You can take a bus from nearby cities like Chennai, Trichy, or Madurai to reach Thanjavur. From the Thanjavur bus stand, you can hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or take a local bus to reach the Punnai Nallur Mariamman Temple. The temple is located approximately 9 kilometers from the bus stand.

Once you reach the vicinity of Punnai Nallur, you can ask the locals for directions to the Mariamman Temple. It is a popular and well-known temple in the area, so most locals will be able to guide you.
