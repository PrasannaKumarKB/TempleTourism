HISTORY

Sankaranainar Kovil, also known as Sankaranarayanaswamy Temple, is a historic Hindu temple located in Thirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva in the form of Sankaranarayana, an amalgamation of Lord Shiva and Lord Vishnu. The temple is known for its architectural grandeur and ancient sculptures. With a rich history that spans several centuries, the Sankaranainar Kovil is a revered pilgrimage site for devotees.
