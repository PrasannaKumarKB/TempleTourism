HISTORY

The Adiratneswarar Temple is a sacred Hindu temple located in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is believed to have been established by Lord Rama. The temple is known for its historical significance and is considered one of the holiest places in Hindu mythology. Devotees visit this temple to seek blessings from Lord Shiva and for spiritual solace.
