HISTORY

Eachanari Vinayagar Temple, located in Coimbatore, is a popular Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It is one of the oldest and most revered temples in the region. The temple is known for its grand architecture and exquisite stone carvings. The presiding deity, Lord Vinayagar (Ganesha), is believed to be a powerful deity who blesses his devotees with wisdom, prosperity, and success.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Coimbatore is Coimbatore International Airport.From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Eachanari Temple, which is approximately 23 kilometers away.

By Train: Coimbatore Junction is the main railway station in Coimbatore.From the railway station, you can hire a taxi, take an auto-rickshaw, or use public transportation to reach Eachanari Temple, which is approximately 16 kilometers away.

By Road: Coimbatore is well-connected by road, and you can reach the city by bus or private vehicle.From Coimbatore, you can take the Pollachi Road (NH 209) towards Pollachi. Eachanari Temple is located on Pollachi Road, approximately 10 kilometers from the city center.

By Taxi or Autorickshaw: You can hire a taxi, take an auto-rickshaw, or use public transportation to reach the temple.
