HISTORY

The Adhi Jaganatha Perumal Temple is a sacred Hindu temple located in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Jaganatha Perumal, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The temple has a rich history and is known for its architectural beauty and spiritual significance. Devotees visit this temple to seek blessings, peace, and fulfillment of their wishes.

WAYS TO REACH

By Road: Thirupullani is located in the Ramanathapuram district of Tamil Nadu. You can reach Thirupullani by road from nearby cities. If you are coming from Rameshwaram, you can take the Ramanathapuram-Rameshwaram Road and continue towards Thirupullani. The temple is located in the heart of Thirupullani, and you can easily find it by asking the locals or following the signboards.

By Train: The nearest railway station to Thirupullani is Ramanathapuram Railway Station, which is well-connected to major cities in Tamil Nadu. From the railway station, you can hire a taxi or an auto-rickshaw to reach Thirupullani, which is approximately 20 kilometers away.

By Bus: Thirupullani has good bus connectivity with nearby towns and cities. State-owned and private buses operate regular services to Thirupullani. You can check the bus schedules and board a bus to reach Thirupullani. Once you arrive at the Thirupullani bus stand, the Adhi Jaganatha Temple is within walking distance.

By Private Vehicle: If you have access to a private vehicle, you can drive to Thirupullani. The roads leading to Thirupullani are well-maintained, and there are parking facilities available near the temple.
