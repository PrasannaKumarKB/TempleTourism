HISTORY

Kodandaramar Temple, also known as Erode Temple, is a famous Hindu temple located in Erode, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Rama, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu, along with his consort Sita Devi and brother Lakshmana. The temple is renowned for its exquisite architecture and intricate carvings, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the region. Devotees flock to this temple to seek the blessings of Lord Rama and to experience the divine atmosphere.
