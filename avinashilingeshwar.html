HISTORY

Located in the town of Avinashi, Avinashi Lingeswarar Temple is a divine abode dedicated to Lord Shiva. This ancient temple showcases exquisite Dravidian architecture and holds immense religious significance. The presiding deity, Lord Lingeswarar, is revered for his benevolence and is believed to fulfill the prayers of devotees.
