HISTORY

Thiru Thanjai Mamanikoil Divyadesam located on the banks of a river Vennaru, about 5 km from Thanjavur, Tamilnadu, which is a group of 3 temples. Sreeman Narayanan is blessing in the three different temples as Sri Neelamaga Perumal, Thiru Manikundra Perumal and Veera Narasimha Perumal. These three temples are being collectively worshipped as One Divyadesam. Among the 108 Divyadesam, Thiru Thanjai Mamanikoil is the One and only Divyadesam where three temples located very nearby, in a walkable distance, are put together worshipped as one Divyadesam. Thiru Thanjai Mamanikoil located on the way to Kumbakonam from Thanjavur
