HISTORY

Bannari Amman Temple is a renowned Hindu temple located in Sathyamangalam, Erode, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Goddess Bannari Amman, who is worshipped as the guardian deity of the region. The temple has a rich history and holds immense religious significance. It is known for its spiritual atmosphere and attracts a large number of devotees. The temple showcases magnificent architecture and intricate sculptures that depict various mythological stories.
