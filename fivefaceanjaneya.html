HISTORY

The Five-Faced Hanuman Temple is a sacred Hindu temple located in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Hanuman, the monkey deity known for his devotion and strength. The temple is believed to be the place where Lord Hanuman revealed his five-faced form (Panchamukha Anjaneya) to showcase his divine powers. Devotees visit this temple to seek blessings, protection, and strength from Lord Hanuman.
