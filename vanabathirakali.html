HISTORY

Vanabathirakali Amman Temple, situated in Mettupalayam, Tamil Nadu, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Goddess Vanabathirakali. The temple holds immense spiritual significance and is known for its historical and cultural heritage. Devotees visit the temple to seek blessings and offer their prayers.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Mettupalayam is Coimbatore International Airport, which is approximately 45 kilometers away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Vanabathirakali Amman Temple.

By Train: Mettupalayam has its own railway station, known as Mettupalayam Railway Station. You can reach Mettupalayam by train and then hire a taxi, take an auto-rickshaw, or use public transportation to reach Vanabathirakali Amman Temple.

By Road: Mettupalayam is well-connected by road. You can reach the town by bus or private vehicle. Vanabathirakali Amman Temple is located in the heart of Mettupalayam, and you can easily access it.

By Taxi or Rickshaw: You can hire a taxi or take an auto-rickshaw to reach Vanabathirakali Amman Temple within Mettupalayam.
