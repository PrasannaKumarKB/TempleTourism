HISTORY

Kayathar Nagammal Koil, also known as Nagammal Temple, is a prominent Hindu temple located in Kayathar, Thirunelveli. It is dedicated to Goddess Nagammal, a revered deity in the region. The temple holds immense religious significance and is believed to have a history dating back several centuries. The temple's architecture and intricate carvings showcase the rich cultural heritage of the area. Devotees visit the Kayathar Nagammal Koil to seek the blessings of the goddess and participate in various religious ceremonies and festivals.
