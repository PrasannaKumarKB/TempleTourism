HISTORY

Kottaiyur Kodeeswarar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Kottaiyur, a village in the outskirts of Kumbakonam, in Thanjavur district in Tamil Nadu, India. Shiva is worshipped as Koteeswarar and His consort Parvathi as Pandhadu Nayaki. Lord Koteeswarar is revered in the 7th century Tamil Saiva canonical and greatest work, Tevaram, written by Tamil saint poets known as the Nayanmars and classified as Paadal Petra Sthalam, the 275 temples revered in the canon. The temple has a five-tiered Raja Gopuram, the entrance tower and all the shrines are enclosed in rectangular walls. The temple has four daily rituals at various times from 6:00 a.m. to 8 p.m., and three yearly festivals on its calendar, namely Margaḻi Tiruvathirai during the Tamil month of Margaḻi (December–January), Shivaratri in February–March, Panguni Uthiram during Panguni (March–April) and Arrow festival during Purattasi (September–October) being the most prominent.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Thanjavur is Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ), located approximately 66 kilometers away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Thanjavur. Once in Thanjavur, you can further hire a taxi or take a local bus to reach Kodeeswarar Temple.

By Train: Thanjavur has its own railway station called Thanjavur Junction, which is well-connected to major cities in India. From the railway station, you can hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or take a local bus to reach Kodeeswarar Temple. The temple is located about 3 kilometers from the railway station.

By Road: Thanjavur is well-connected by road and has good bus connectivity. You can take a bus from nearby cities like Chennai, Trichy, or Madurai to reach Thanjavur. From the Thanjavur bus stand, you can hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or take a local bus to reach Kodeeswarar Temple. The temple is located approximately 4 kilometers from the bus stand.

Once you reach Thanjavur, you can ask the locals for directions to Kodeeswarar Temple. It is a prominent temple in the area, so most locals will be able to guide you.
