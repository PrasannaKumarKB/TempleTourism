HISTORY

Nellaiappar Temple, also known as Arulmigu Swami Nellaiappar and Kanthimathi Ambal Temple, is a renowned Hindu temple located in Thirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva (Nellaiappar) and Goddess Parvati (Kanthimathi Ambal). The temple has a rich history that dates back over 2000 years and is considered one of the largest temples in Tamil Nadu.
