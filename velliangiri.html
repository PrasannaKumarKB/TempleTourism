HISTORY

Velliangiri Andavar Temple, also known as Velliangiri Hill Temple, is a renowned Hindu temple located on the outskirts of Coimbatore. Situated at the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains, this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, known as Velliangiri Andavar.The temple is nestled amidst scenic natural beauty, surrounded by lush greenery and picturesque landscapes. It is a popular pilgrimage destination for devotees seeking spiritual solace and enlightenment. The trek to the temple is considered a sacred journey, as it involves climbing the Velliangiri Hills and traversing through a well-marked trail.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Coimbatore is Coimbatore International Airport.From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Velliangiri Temple, which is approximately 42 kilometers away.

By Train: Coimbatore Junction is the main railway station in Coimbatore.From the railway station, you can hire a taxi, take an auto-rickshaw, or use public transportation to reach Velliangiri Temple, which is approximately 40 kilometers away.

By Road: Coimbatore is well-connected by road, and you can reach the city by bus or private vehicle.From Coimbatore, you can take the Coimbatore-Pollachi Road (NH 209) towards Pollachi.Velliangiri Temple is located at the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains, near Poondi. After reaching Poondi, you can follow the signs or ask the locals for directions to reach Velliangiri Temple.
