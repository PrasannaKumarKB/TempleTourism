HISTORY

The Thirupullani Temple is a sacred Hindu temple located in Ramanathapuram, Tamil Nadu. It is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is believed to be one of the 108 Divya Desams, which are the holy abodes of Lord Vishnu. The temple is associated with the legend of Lord Rama performing rituals here to seek the blessings of Lord Varuna (the god of rain) before his journey to Lanka. Devotees visit this temple to seek divine blessings and for the fulfillment of their wishes.
