HISTORY

Arulmigu Solaimalai Murugan Temple is a Hindu temple, located about 25 kilometres north of Madurai, Tamilnadu, India atop a hill covered with dense forests. One of the six important abodes (Arupadaiveedu) of Lord Muruga of Madurai District Entrance of the temple come under Madurai And Entire Prahara Belong to Madurai District of Tamilnadu, it is close to the Vishnu temple of Azhagar Kovil.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Madurai is the Madurai International Airport (IXM). From the airport, you can hire a taxi or use public transportation to reach the temple. The Pazhamudhir Cholai Temple is located approximately 20 kilometers away from the airport, and the journey takes around 40-50 minutes by road.

By Train: Madurai Junction is the primary railway station in Madurai and is well-connected to various cities in India. You can take a train to Madurai and then hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or use public transport to reach the temple. The temple is situated about 10 kilometers away from the railway station, and it takes around 30 minutes to reach by road.

By Bus: Madurai has an extensive bus transportation system, with both state-run and private buses connecting different cities and towns. You can take a bus to Madurai and get down at the Periyar Bus Stand or the Mattuthavani Integrated Bus Terminus. From there, you can hire a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or use local buses to reach the Pazhamudhir Cholai Temple. The temple is located about 12 kilometers away from the bus stands, and the journey may take around 30-40 minutes by road.

By Car: If you prefer to drive, you can reach Madurai by road via national highways. The city is well-connected to major cities and towns in South India. You can use navigation apps or maps to find the best route to the Pazhamudhir Cholai Temple.

Once you reach Madurai, you can ask locals or use GPS navigation to guide you to the temple. The Pazhamudhir Cholai Temple is a famous religious site in Madurai, and people in the area will be able to direct you to its location.
