ATTRACTIVE TOURIST PLACES IN AND AROUND COIMBATORE

Dhyanalinga Temple

A unique meditative space located in the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains, providing a powerful energy center for meditation and inner well-being.

Adiyogi Statue

The Adiyogi Statue is a magnificent 112-feet tall statue of Lord Shiva, located at the Isha Yoga Center in Coimbatore. It is considered the largest bust sculpture in the world and is a symbol of yoga and meditation.

Ooty

A popular hill station located approximately 85 kilometers from Coimbatore, known for its scenic beauty, tea plantations, botanical gardens, and the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

Velliangiri Hills

A range of hills known for its natural beauty and spiritual significance, located on the outskirts of Coimbatore. It is a popular destination for trekking and pilgrimage.

Siruvani Dam and Waterfalls

A scenic dam and waterfalls located in the Siruvani Hills, about 37 kilometers from Coimbatore. It is known for its picturesque beauty and serves as a source of drinking water for the city.

Monkey Falls

A natural waterfall situated near Pollachi, about 75 kilometers from Coimbatore, known for its lush greenery and serene ambiance.

Valparai

A beautiful hill station located in the Anaimalai Hills, about 100 kilometers from Coimbatore. It offers breathtaking views, tea estates, and a tranquil atmosphere.

Parambikulam Tiger Reserve

A wildlife sanctuary and tiger reserve situated near Pollachi, about 100 kilometers from Coimbatore, offering wildlife safaris and nature trails.

Anamalai Tiger Reserve

A wildlife sanctuary located in the Anaimalai Hills, approximately 90 kilometers from Coimbatore, known for its diverse flora and fauna, including tigers, elephants, and numerous bird species.

Kovai Kondattam

An amusement park and water park in Coimbatore, featuring thrilling rides, water slides, and entertainment options for visitors of all ages.

Black Thunder Theme Park

A popular theme park in Mettupalayam, about 40 kilometers from Coimbatore, offering a variety of water rides, dry rides, and adventure activities.

Aliyar Dam

A picturesque dam located in the Aliyar village, about 65 kilometers from Coimbatore. It offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and serves as a popular picnic spot.

Thirumoorthy Dam

A scenic dam situated in the foothills of the Thirumoorthy Hills, approximately 80 kilometers from Coimbatore. It is known for its serene atmosphere and serves as a source of irrigation.

Thirumoorthy Falls

A captivating waterfall located near the Thirumoorthy Dam, offering a refreshing experience amidst the lush greenery. It is a popular spot for nature lovers and trekkers.
