HISTORY

Kailasanathar Temple, also known as Thirumayam Temple, is a historic Hindu temple located in Thirumayam, Trichy. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is renowned for its architectural beauty and intricate carvings. The temple holds great religious significance and attracts devotees from far and wide. With a rich history that dates back several centuries, the Kailasanathar Temple stands as a testament to Dravidian architecture and showcases the craftsmanship of the artisans of that era.
