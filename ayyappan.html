HISTORY

The Sree Ayyappan Temple in Coimbatore is a sacred abode dedicated to Lord Ayappan, also known as Harihara Putra. The temple carries a rich history that dates back several centuries. It serves as a symbol of devotion and unity, attracting thousands of devotees each year. The temple's architecture showcases intricate carvings and sculptures, depicting various episodes from Lord Ayappan's life. Inside the temple, the sanctum sanctorum houses a majestic idol of Lord Ayappan, adorned with vibrant decorations. The serene ambiance and the divine presence create a spiritual atmosphere, providing solace and blessings to all who visit.
