HISTORY

Samayapuram Mariamman Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Mariamman. Located in Samayapuram, near Trichy, it is a significant pilgrimage site in Tamil Nadu. The temple is known for its beautiful architecture and vibrant festivals. Devotees from all over the country visit the temple to seek the blessings of the powerful Goddess Mariamman.

WAYS TO REACH

By Air: The nearest airport to Trichy is Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ). From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Samayapuram Mariamman Temple, which is approximately 25 kilometers away.

By Auto-rickshaw or Taxi: The most convenient way to reach Samayapuram Mariamman Temple is by hiring an auto-rickshaw or a taxi. The temple is located in Samayapuram, which is approximately 20 kilometers from Trichy. You can find auto-rickshaws and taxis easily in Trichy, and they can take you directly to the temple entrance.

By Bus: Trichy has a well-developed bus network, and you can take a local bus to reach Samayapuram Mariamman Temple. Look for buses that are heading towards Samayapuram or Tollgate, as these buses will pass by or near the temple. You can board the bus from the nearest bus stop and inform the conductor or driver that you want to get off at Samayapuram Mariamman Temple.

By Own Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Samayapuram Mariamman Temple. The temple is located off the Trichy-Chennai Highway (NH38). You can use GPS navigation or follow signboards to reach the temple.
